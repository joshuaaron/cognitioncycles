We invest our time in getting to know our customers cycling needs to ensure you experience the fun and enjoyment of riding as much as we do.

At Cognition Cycles, we believe everyone should have a safe and enjoyable riding experience. We offer a range of cycling products and services aimed at assisting all of our customers from the first time rider through to the experienced cyclist.

We are happy to assist by answering your questions and providing advice on all things cycling. This can be in relation to your current bike and riding needs, or of course if you are seeking new bikes, parts, accessories, clothing or general riding advice and tips.

Feel free to contact us or simply swing by the store for a chat. You are also welcome to bring your bike in for an obligation free assessment on servicing, upgrades, fitting or adjustments.
